FAQ

What specialties does Sanchi Hospital treat?

Sanchi Hospital treats a wide range of specialties, including general medicine, neuro medicine, neuro surgery , pediatrics, gynecology, and emergency Medicine

How can I make an appointment with a specialist?

You can easily make an appointment by calling our reception desk or using our online booking system on the website.

What emergency services are available at Sanchi Hospital?

We provide 24/7 emergency and trauma care, ensuring immediate attention in critical situations at any time.

Does the hospital offer support for patients and their families?

Yes, we provide support services such as counseling, patient education, and help for families during their hospital visit.

How does Sanchi Hospital ensure patient safety and infection control?

We follow strict infection control measures, keep the hospital clean and safe, and train our staff regularly to protect patients.
